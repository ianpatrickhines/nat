Frequently Asked Questions

How does Nat connect to my NationBuilder?

Nat uses NationBuilder's official OAuth system. You'll authorize Nat to access your nation, and all actions are performed as your NationBuilder user - not a separate "Nat" account.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. Your NationBuilder credentials are encrypted and stored securely. Nat never stores your supporter data - it only accesses it when you ask a question, through NationBuilder's API.

What counts as a query?

Each message you send to Nat counts as one query. If Nat needs to use multiple tools to answer your question, that still counts as just one query.

What happens if I hit my query limit?

You'll see a message letting you know you've reached your limit. You can upgrade your plan at any time to continue using Nat, or wait until your next billing cycle.

Can Nat delete or modify data?

Yes, but Nat always asks for confirmation before performing destructive actions like deleting records or making bulk changes. You stay in control.

What browsers are supported?

Nat is currently available as a Chrome extension. Support for Firefox, Safari, and Edge is planned for future releases.
