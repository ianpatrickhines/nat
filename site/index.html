Questions & Answers

How does Nat connect to my NationBuilder?

Nat uses NationBuilder's official OAuth system. You authorize Nat once, and all actions are performed as your NationBuilder user — not a separate "Nat" account. Your team's existing permissions still apply.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Your NationBuilder credentials are encrypted and stored in AWS Secrets Manager. Nat never stores your supporter data — it only accesses it when you ask a question, through NationBuilder's API. No PII is logged or displayed beyond name and city/state.

What counts as a query?

Each message you send to Nat counts as one query. If Nat needs to use multiple API calls to answer your question (like searching, then tagging), that still counts as just one query.

Can Nat delete or modify my data?

Yes, but Nat always asks for confirmation before performing destructive actions like deleting records or making bulk changes. You can also say "undo that" to reverse recent actions within your session.

Does everyone on my nation get access?

Yes. Nat is billed per nation, not per user. Anyone who can log into your NationBuilder control panel can use Nat. Usage counts against a shared monthly query pool.

What browsers are supported?

Nat is currently available as a Chrome extension. It works on any page under *.nationbuilder.com. Firefox and Edge support is planned.

What happens if I hit my query limit?

On the Nat plan (500/mo), you'll see a message when you've reached your limit. You can upgrade to Nat Pro for unlimited queries, or wait until your next billing cycle. The counter resets automatically.
