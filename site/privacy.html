Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 12, 2025

1. Introduction

Hines Digital LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates Nat, an AI-powered assistant for NationBuilder users. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our Service.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly. By using Nat, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you subscribe to Nat, we collect:

  • Email address (provided via Stripe checkout)
  • Payment information (processed and stored by Stripe, not by us)
  • Subscription plan and billing details

2.2 NationBuilder Connection

When you connect your NationBuilder account, we receive and store:

  • OAuth access tokens (encrypted and stored securely)
  • OAuth refresh tokens (encrypted and stored securely)
  • Your NationBuilder nation slug (e.g., "yourorg.nationbuilder.com")
  • Token expiration timestamps

2.3 Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • Query count for billing purposes
  • Timestamp of last query (for rate limiting)
  • Error logs for debugging and service improvement

2.4 Query Data

When you submit queries to Nat:

  • Your query text is sent to our servers for processing
  • Queries are processed by Claude (Anthropic's AI) to generate responses
  • We do NOT persistently store your queries or the NationBuilder data returned
  • Query content is only held in memory during processing

2.5 NationBuilder Data

Important: Nat accesses your NationBuilder data to process queries, but:

  • We do NOT store copies of your NationBuilder supporter data
  • Data is fetched in real-time from NationBuilder's API when needed
  • Data passes through our systems only during query processing
  • We have no access to your data outside of active query sessions

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide the Service: Process your queries and connect to your NationBuilder account
  • Manage Subscriptions: Handle billing, enforce query limits, and manage your account
  • Maintain Security: Protect against unauthorized access and fraud
  • Improve the Service: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and performance
  • Communicate: Send important service updates, security alerts, and support responses
  • Comply with Law: Meet legal obligations and respond to lawful requests

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

4.1 Service Providers

We share data with trusted third-party service providers:

  • Stripe: Processes payments and stores payment information. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic (Claude): Processes AI queries. Query content is sent to Anthropic's API. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS): Hosts our infrastructure. See AWS Privacy Policy.
  • NationBuilder: Your NationBuilder data is accessed via their API under your authorization. See NationBuilder's Privacy Policy.

4.2 We Do NOT

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your NationBuilder data with other Nat users
  • Use your data for advertising or marketing by third parties
  • Share data with third parties for their own purposes

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request, or if necessary to protect our rights, safety, or property.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS/HTTPS) and at rest
  • Token Security: NationBuilder OAuth tokens are stored in AWS Secrets Manager with encryption
  • Access Control: Strict access controls limit who can access infrastructure
  • Secure Infrastructure: Hosted on AWS with SOC 2 and ISO 27001 compliance
  • Regular Updates: We promptly apply security patches and updates

While we implement strong security measures, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for up to 30 days after cancellation
  • OAuth Tokens: Deleted immediately when you disconnect NationBuilder or cancel your account
  • Usage Logs: Retained for 90 days for debugging and service improvement
  • Payment Records: Retained as required by law (typically 7 years for tax purposes)
  • Query Content: Not persistently stored; only held in memory during processing

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Portability

You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

7.2 Correction

You can update your account information or request corrections by contacting us.

7.3 Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated data. Note that some data may be retained as required by law.

7.4 Disconnect NationBuilder

You can revoke Nat's access to your NationBuilder account at any time through NationBuilder's authorized applications settings. This immediately invalidates our stored tokens.

7.5 Cancel Subscription

You can cancel your subscription at any time. Your data will be retained for 30 days in case you wish to resubscribe, then deleted.

7.6 Opt-Out of Communications

You can opt out of non-essential communications by contacting us. You cannot opt out of critical service communications (security alerts, billing issues).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@asknat.ai.

8. International Data Transfers

Our servers are located in the United States. If you are accessing the Service from outside the US, your data will be transferred to and processed in the US. By using the Service, you consent to this transfer.

We comply with applicable data protection laws when transferring data internationally.

9. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Legal Basis: We process your data based on contract performance (providing the Service), legitimate interests (improving the Service), and consent (where applicable).
  • Right to Object: You can object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to Restrict: You can request restriction of processing in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: You can file a complaint with your local data protection authority.
  • Data Protection Officer: For GDPR inquiries, contact privacy@asknat.ai.

10. CCPA Compliance (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Know: Request information about data we collect and how it's used.
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Right to Opt-Out: We do NOT sell personal information, so this right does not apply.
  • Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@asknat.ai.

11. Children's Privacy

Nat is not intended for use by children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 18, we will delete it promptly.

12. Chrome Extension Permissions

The Nat Chrome extension requests the following permissions:

  • Host Permissions (*.nationbuilder.com): Required to inject the Nat sidebar on NationBuilder pages
  • Storage: Used to store authentication state locally in your browser
  • Active Tab: Used to detect the current NationBuilder page context
  • Alarms: Used to maintain background connections during streaming

The extension does not access data from other websites or tabs.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Sending an email to the address associated with your account
  • Displaying a notice in the Nat extension

Continued use of the Service after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

For GDPR-related inquiries, please include "GDPR Request" in the subject line.
